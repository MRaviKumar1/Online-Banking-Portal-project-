HCI Design Principles Applied

Nielsen H1 – Visibility of StatusReal-time server status, transaction progress bars, and session timer keep users informed at all times.
Nielsen H5 – Error PreventionReal-time input validation, beneficiary pre-selection, and amount preset buttons prevent errors before submission.
Nielsen H6 – Recognition over RecallSaved beneficiaries with names/avatars, amount presets, and biometric login reduce cognitive load.
Nielsen H9 – Help Diagnose ErrorsSpecific error messages explain exactly what went wrong and how to fix it — no generic 'Transaction Failed'.
Norman – FeedbackEvery action produces immediate visual feedback: toasts, progress bars, and confirmation screens.
Shneiderman – Reduce Short-Term Memory3-step wizard with visible step tracking, confirmation review screen, and OTP auto-advance reduce mental effort.
Shneiderman – Prevent ErrorsSession timeout warning with one-click extension prevents loss of entered data during long transactions.
